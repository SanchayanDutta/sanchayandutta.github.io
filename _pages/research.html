Current Research

[UC Davis, 2021 - *] My current research focus with Greg is studying anyonic software fault-tolerance and quantum algebra, especially but not only in relation to Turaev-Viro codes. I'm also interested in investigating the landscape of algebraic geometry quantum codes and comparing it to the recent progress in quantum LDPC codes.
[Summer 2023] I'm hosted by Anurag Anshu at Harvard University and working on establishing a connection between explicit lower bounds on SoS instances and the NLTS theorem. With Suvrit Sra, at TU Munich, I'm working on the mathematics of linear transformers. Previously, I worked with his group on Riemannian bilevel optimization. This is part of my side interest in machine learning.
